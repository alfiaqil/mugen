---------------<Character Info>------------------

(**Note: 1-10 Ranking System: 1 = Lowest, 10 = Best)

 - Intelligence: 6
 - Strength: 6
 - Speed: 6
 - Stamina: 6
 - Energy Projection: 1
 - Fighting Ability: 6

The "jack of all trades" and the main protagonist of MUGEN, Kung Fu Man. 
He may not particularly excel at anything, but he is the core basis of fighting in the MUGEN universe/engine. 
A solid, well balanced character all around. Kung Fu Man can defintely hold his own against any foe. 
While he may not excel in any trait, he has no weaknesses either.

-----------------<Movelist>----------------------

 U = Up
 D = Down
 F = Forward
 B = Back
 UF = Up Forward
 UB = Up Back
 DF = Down Forward
 DB = Down Back
 LP = Light Punch
 MP = Medium Punch
 HP = Heavy Punch
 LK = Light Kick
 MK = Medium Kick
 HK = Heavy Kick
 S = Start

 (Air) - Move must be performed in the air.
 (EX)  - Move has an EX version, performed by pressing two punch/kick buttons.
 (MAX) - Use two punch/kick buttons when performing a Super move to power it up.


<NORMAL>

 Kung Fu Throw				F(or B) + any 2 punches (grab)
 Taunt					S					

<SPECIALS>

 Kung Fu Palm(EX) 			D, DF, F, + any punch

 Kung Fu Zankou(EX)			D, DF, F, + any kick

<SUPERS>

 Kung Fu Barrage(MAX)		        D, DF, F(x2), + any punch
  
<LV3 SUPERS>

 Grand Kung Fu Upper			D, DB, B,(x2) + any 2 punches

----------------<Move Details>-------------------

 - (EX)Kung Fu Blow can negate projectiles
 - EX Kung Fu Palm bounces foes off walls, allowing you to follow up with certain attacks
 - EX/Grand Kung Fu Upper can not be blocked while in the air
 - EX Kung Fu Knee/(EX)Kung Fu Upper has some invulnerability during their respective start ups
 - EX Kung Fu Zankou can resist normal/special projectiles
  
---------------<Gameplay Notes>------------------

COMBO SYSTEM:
 - Most Normal standing/crouching attacks can be canceled into Special and Super moves
 - Some Special moves can be canceled into Super moves
 - Performing a Super/MAX Cancel resets the juggle points but also reduces damage dealt
   
MAX MODE:
 - Your attack and defense is increased while in MAX Mode
 - EX moves can be performed at no power cost, though take away from time sustained in MAX mode
 - All Lv1 supers can be performed at no power cost, though immediately uses up all time in MAX mode
 - All Lv2 and higher supers will cost 1 power bar, along with using up all time in MAX mode
 - Even without a power bar, as long as you have time left in MAX mode, you can perform Lv1 supers/EX moves
 - You can not Power Charge/gain power while in MAX mode
 - You can not perform a Zero Counter while in MAX Mode
