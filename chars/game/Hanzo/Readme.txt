--| Hanzo by Antagonist |------------------------------------------

Yatagarasu character for M.U.G.E.N.
Custom version
This character is Open Source. Feel free to use any part of it or modify it. 

--| Commands |-----------------------------------------------------

   U	  A - Light Kick	B - Strong Kick
 B   F
   D	  X - Light Punch	Y - Strong Punch


--| System Related Commands |--------------------------------------

 Forward Dash		Tap FF
 Back Dash		Tap BB
 Throw			a + x
 Leap Attack		x + y
 Guard Break Attack	b + y
 Hip Launch             a + b
 Standing Parry		Tap F when attacked
 Crouching Parry	Tap D when attacked low
 Air Parry		Tap F when attacked in air
 Wall Jump		UF at the edge of the screen during a jump


--| Dir Moves |----------------------------------------------------

 Lunging Side Kick	F + a
 High Roundhouse Kick	F + b
 Dive kick		(In Air) DF + a or b (Must be done at the peak of a jump,
			and can't be done on backward jump)
 Sweep			DF + b


--| Special Moves |------------------------------------------------

 Saiunricchu Mozuotoshi		Hold D,U + x or y
 Kikkajyunikou Jibashiri	B,DB,D,DF,F + x or y
 Gekkouippa Makigeri		D,DB,B + a or b
 Utsusemi			D,D during a high a jump


--| EX Moves (Requires half powerbar) |----------------------------

 Saiunricchu Mozuotoshi EX	Hold D,U + x+y		Does more damage than normal version
 Kikkajyunikou Jibashiri EX	B,DB,D,DF,F + x+y	Can teleport behind opponent
 Gekkouippa Makigeri EX		D,DB,B + a+b		Hits 4 times instead of 1


--| Super Moves (Requires one powerbar) |--------------------------

 Shinshinmumyou Togihotaru	D,DF,F,D,DF,F + a or b
 Tenraigin Kuchinawaichigo	D,U,D,U + x or y


--| Notes |--------------------------------------------------------

 * Due to her huge size sprites, combos may not work against certain characters. You can
   change her x and y scale to a lower value. ".97" or ".96" it's a good value for me.
 * If you perform "Tenraigin Kuchinawaichigo" too far from the opponent, she'll teleport
   in front of the opponent and grab the wrong way.
